Project Stage 1

Summary

In this stage, you:

  • choose a data-driven problem and do preliminary requirement analysis,
  • create an ER model for the database serving the solution to your problem, and
  • convert your ER model to a relational database desing.
Your deliverables are:
  • an ER diagram of your database's conceptual design, and
  • a relational database design created based on the above ER diagram.
